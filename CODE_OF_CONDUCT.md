# Code of Conduct

Iris aims to be a practical, respectful project space.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept corrections quickly.
- Keep discussion focused on the project and its users.
- Give actionable feedback with evidence when possible.
- Protect private data, credentials, logs, and local-only context.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information or secrets.
- Repeatedly derailing project discussions.
- Abusive, discriminatory, or sexualized language or imagery.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues or pull requests; or block participants when needed to keep the project safe and useful.

If you need to report a conduct issue, use a private contact path when available and include links or screenshots that help maintainers understand the situation.
